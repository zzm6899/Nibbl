Save the photo

Take a photo or share one into Nibbl. The app cuts out the food or drink and places it on your calendar.

02

Tag the day

Add cafe, caffeine, category, and friends. Friends are added by invite link or public username.

03

Share carefully

A public day link shows synced logs for that friend on one calendar date. Anyone with the link can view it.

What links mean

Friend links add a real Nibbl friend tag to the app. Public day links open a web page for one calendar day. They are different on purpose.
